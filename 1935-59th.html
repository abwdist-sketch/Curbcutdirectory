Property Notes

The available DOB records make the current legal status of this curb cut appear questionable. DOB records show that a 2014 Alteration Type 3 application was filed for a new 10-foot-wide curb cut at this property, and the application record indicates that the job was approved and that a permit was issued under Permit/Job #321003123.

However, later DOB records tied to the same permit/job number show additional issues. The application history shows a “Notice to Revoke,” and a later DOB violation states that the Borough Commissioner ordered all work stopped under Permit #321003123 due to applicant withdrawal. The violation record also appears to list that DOB violation as active.

Because those later DOB records are connected to the same curb-cut permit, this page does not treat the curb cut as clearly confirmed or fully authorized. The records suggest that the curb cut may not be currently legally authorized, or that its authorization may have been affected by the later revocation/stop-work history.

At the same time, this page is not making a final legal determination. The records available online do not, by themselves, conclusively prove the final legal status of the physical curb cut. A final determination would require further confirmation from DOB, including whether the permit was ever revoked, reinstated, signed off, superseded, or otherwise resolved.
